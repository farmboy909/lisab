Lisa B Originals is a manufacturer of mastectomy products, based in Regina, Saskatchewan. We create the most life-like breast prostheses from 100% silicone composites. Our non-custom stock products are available in a full range of sizes, in both full and slight styles, with options in five skin tone variations. All Lisa B Originals products are hand crafted and each piece is an “original” work of art. Our products are covered by a two year warranty. We offer 3-5 day shipping from time of order with no minimum order.

This website is solely for dealers ordering our product and for prerequisite training. We do not sell our products on line, however you may learn more about our products and distributors by viewing this web-site.

Lisa B Originals provides extensive training in the fitting of our products by a B.O.C. accredited Fitter – the only one in western Canada with these credentials. We provide follow-up sessions every six months or as needed. We are an AADL (Alberta) approved manufacturer. We are very passionate about our products and services and we offer our clients 100% satisfaction.
