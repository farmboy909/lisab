Introduction

 

At the age of 36 years my aunt, a beautiful young lady, succumbed to breast cancer. This was my first exposure to the terror of this disease. I was ten years old. Sixteen years ago my close friend lost a breast and I found out the next day. I thought she was “only” going to have a lumpectomy. She and I tease each other frequently, and I told her we should have made a copy of her breast so that at some time in the future we could “put it back on”. At the start of 2010 my sister-in-law Lisa had both breasts removed and for some reason it profoundly affected me. Thoughts of my friend came back to me, and gave me the direction to which my life is now consumed. Since then, with much help from my wife and from many fabulous women who became LisaB ‘models’, LisaB has developed the ultimate breast prosthesis. Lisa B Originals Inc. officially opened its doors June 1, 2011. One client commented that one day, while relaxing on her patio and reading a book, she looked down at her chest and forgot that she ever had breast cancer. All women should feel like that.

Our Products

 

Lisa B Originals’ products are created by hand in Saskatchewan, Canada. Made of 100% silicone into a lightweight, life-like breast prosthesis. Each is an individual work of original art:
  • Skin color, freckles and veins are intrinsic, which means they are built in below the surface and will not wear off.

  • The non-slip surface ensures they do not move around within a properly fitted bra, and definitely won’t pop out.

  • They are designed to fit against the chest wall in a regular style bra (non-mastectomy) with comfort.

  • They are completely water-proof and safe for swimming with no effect on the product.

  • They will not leak any substance when punctured.

  • The LisaB prostheses are heat and moisture resistant.

  • The Lisa B Originals manufacturer’s two-year guarantee closely matches the renewal period through most group insurance and government benefit plans.
